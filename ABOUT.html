ABOUT US

The Arra J Restobar Studio specialized in good services offered and amazing place to chill and hang out. Arra J serves spirited, Filipino-Italian influenced food that reflects a longstanding commitment to local and international cuisine. Our food are connections to our family traditions that the experience at Arra J showcases our focus on procuring the highest quality food and wine, we are, above all, committed to making our industry a more equitable and sustainable home for all of those who work and come in it. We Offers delicious food made from premium ingredients, wine and fresh drinks special and more. Don't miss out!

STORY

Before Arra J Restobar is small food business that only offers grilled food with drinks outside the backyard that run by the Gastador siblings. It only accommodate minimum of twenty customers every nigth.Only the siblings do all the services that accept walk in, dine in and take outs. It just like an eating place with no special events offers. Years after, the business becomes popular and progress, we moved in a more conventient spot, take inovation from food we offer and hired a professional chief.
